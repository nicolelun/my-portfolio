But they that wait upon the Lord shall renew their strength; they shall mount up with wings as eagles; they shall run, and not be weary; and they shall walk, and not faint. — Isaiah 40:31-1

A neighborhood of 3.2 square miles, North Lawndale has over 100 places of worship, many in converted storefronts, residences, and synagogues. Over generations, the Chicago neighborhood has evolved, bearing witness to the lives of its residents and the legacies of history.
